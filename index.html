EcoPoint

A collaborative digital platform connecting consumers and fashion brands to enable local, transparent, and responsible textile recycling.

How EcoPoint Works

For Consumers

Locate nearby textile collection points, track your recycling impact, and earn rewards for responsible disposal of clothing.

For Brands

Access a reliable stream of recyclable textiles, meet sustainability targets, and engage customers through circular initiatives.

For Recyclers

Receive sorted, locally collected textile waste to improve recycling efficiency and reduce transportation emissions.
