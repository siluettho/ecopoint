EcoPoint

A collaborative digital platform connecting consumers and fashion brands to enable local, transparent, and responsible textile recycling.

Project Vision

EcoPoint is designed to address the growing disconnect between clothing consumption and textile waste management. By bringing brands and end-users together through a single mobile platform, the project aims to enable traceable, local recycling pathways while supporting corporate sustainability commitments.

Core Principle: Textile waste collected through EcoPoint remains within national borders and is directed exclusively to verified recycling partners — not incineration or offshore disposal.

How EcoPoint Works

For Consumers

Users locate nearby participating brands, deposit used garments, and track the recycling pathway of their textiles.

For Brands

Brands engage directly with consumers, document recycling outcomes, and strengthen sustainability reporting with verified data.

For Recyclers

Certified recyclers receive sorted, traceable textile streams, improving material recovery efficiency.

Why It Matters

Textile recycling is not only a waste-management challenge but also a climate issue. By prioritizing local processing and textile-to-textile recycling, EcoPoint supports emissions reduction, material circularity, and regulatory alignment.
